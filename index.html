HPC Calculator

version 1

In my previous life as a scientist, I spent a lot of time prepping data for analysis, which is tedious. I decided to automate a part of this process with a desktop application that calculates hippocampal volume & cell density.
Reading and writing to Excel was faciliated with the xlutils module, and the GUI was built using wxPython.
